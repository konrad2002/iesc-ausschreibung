\section{Honors and Awards}

The top three of each rating will receive \textbf{medals}. \textbf{Certificates} will be awarded through sixth place and made available for all places at erzgebirgsschwimmcup.de and in the SwimResults app. For the best men and women performance will be awarded with \textbf{high quality swimwear} and the \textbf{trophy} of the mayor of the Bergstadt of Marienberg. At least 900 DSV points an additional \textbf{cash prize} of \textbf{\awardNinehundretDSV\ euros} will be committed, provided at least 2000 registrations of guest teams exist.  
New hall records are rewarded with \textbf{cash prizes}, \textbf{\awardPoolRecord\ euros}, at 900+ DSV points \textbf{\awardPoolRecordNinehundretDSV\ euros}. The best performance in each age group will be awarded a \textbf{trophy} and a \textbf{swimsuit} or a \textbf{trunk}.  

In all finals at least the following \textbf{cash prizes} will be awarded:	
1st \textbf{\awardAFinalFirst\ euros}, 2nd \textbf{\awardAFinalSecond\ euros}, 3rd \textbf{\awardAFinalThird\ euros}, 4th \textbf{\awardAFinalFourth\ euros}.

At the junior finals (2008 female and 2007 male and younger), providing 2000+ guest registrations, the following \textbf{premiums}: 
1st \textbf{\awardBFinalFirst\ euros}, 2nd \textbf{\awardBFinalSecond\ euros}, 3rd \textbf{\awardBFinalThird\ euros}, 4th \textbf{\awardBFinalFourth\ euros}

Both honors are eternal challenge cups.
