\section{Competition venue}

The competition pool in the „Aqua Marien“ in Marienberg/Erzgebirge is 25 m long. The lanes are separated by wave killer lines. The water depth is 1.40 m to 1.80 m. The water temperature is about 27°C. There is an electronic time measurement system. The swimming area beside the competition is about 50 m long and 15 m wide. The water depth is 2.00 m to 0.10 m.
The water temperature is about 29°C.
