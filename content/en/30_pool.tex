\section{Competition venue}

The competition pool in the „Aqua Marien“ in Marienberg is 25 m long. The four lanes are separated by wave-killer ropes and equipped with Omega OSB 11 starting blocks and backstroke start aids. The water depth is 1.40 m to 1.80 m. The water temperature is about 27 °C. Timing is fully automatic.

The warm-up and cool-down pool is 50 m long and 15 m wide. The water depth ranges from 2.00 m to 0.10 m. The water temperature is about 29 °C.
