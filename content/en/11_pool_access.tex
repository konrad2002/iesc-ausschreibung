\section{Pool Admission Cards}

For all registered athletes, referees and accompanying persons (per 5 athletes 2 tickets for accompanying persons; starting from 11 athletes 4 tickets for accompanying persons, and so on) each association receives free tickets. Additional tickets can be ordered with the registration. The price per day and person is 9 Euros. The price (box office) for adults is 18 Euro and for persons with discount 15 Euro. The teams receive their tickets on arrival. Important!!! Every guest receives a key for which he/she will be responsible. The teams will be held fully liable for damage that their athletes, trainers or accompanying persons have caused.
