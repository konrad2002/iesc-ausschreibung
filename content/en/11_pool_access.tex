\section{Pool Admission Cards}

For all registered athletes, referees and accompanying persons (per 5 athletes 2 tickets for accompanying persons; starting from 11 athletes 4 tickets for accompanying persons, and so on) each association receives free tickets. Additional tickets can be ordered with the registration. The price per day and person is \poolAdmissionCardPrice\ euros. The price (box office) for adults is \poolAdmissionPrice\ euros and for persons with discount \poolAdmissionReducedPrice\ euros. The teams receive their tickets on arrival. Important!!! Every guest receives a key for which he/she will be responsible. The teams will be held fully liable for damage that their athletes, trainers or accompanying persons have caused.
